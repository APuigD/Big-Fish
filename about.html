Our Story

The BigFish Story is suprisingly simple and begins with the collaboration of three "Foodies".

From there, simply add a beautiful historic city full of hidden food gems and BigFish was born! We're proud of all the variety found in the York food scene and it'd be a crime for us not to share it! So browse our website whenever you're stuck for choice on where to eat in Yorkshire's oldest son- our easy to navigate site will send you in the direction of a meal to remember.

Meet The Team!

Blep

Blep is a powerhouse foodie. Coming from a line of foodies, there is no restuarant that Blep doesn't know about! With and expert palette Blep is able to tell outstanding from average at first taste. Blep's reccomended restuarant is Roots- There is no better place in York to try high class food cooked by internationally recognised chefs. But if you're after something more fancy why not try Meltons?

Beans

Beans is a lower of all food's internation- from French to Thai to Indian to box-standard Fish and Chips! But where to get all this in York? Beans reccomends starting at the Shambles Market you never know what you'll stumble across there!

Kitty


Kitty is a simple Foodie and a lover of the good ol' Sunday Roast- That's why kitty recommends the Star Inn the city. Apart from their punny name, they offer a perfect Sunday Roast every Sunday! So why not go down with the family this weekend?
